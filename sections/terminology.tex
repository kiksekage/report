\section*{Terminology}

\textbf{Tweets:} a single sentence, sourced from the website \hyperref{www.twitter.com}, with a maximum length of 280 characters (although tweets used in these datasets will be of a maximum length of 140).\\
\\
\textbf{Regression emotions:} the four emotions that are used in the regression task of the SemEval Task 1 (anger, fear, joy and sadness).\\
